Privacy Policy

GymX Workout Trainer

This Privacy Policy explains how GymX Workout Trainer collects, uses, stores and protects your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Optional mobile number
  • Feedback messages submitted through the app

When We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • Create or sign in to your account.
  • Submit feedback through the app.
  • Contact us for support.
  • Interact with advertisements displayed in the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • Manage user accounts using Firebase Authentication.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Store feedback securely using Google Sheets.
  • Improve our app and services.
  • Process account deletion requests.

How We Protect Your Information

Your personal information is protected using industry-standard security measures. All communication with our services is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology. Access to your information is limited to authorized personnel only.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the application:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) for user registration and login.
  • Google Sheets (Google LLC) for securely storing feedback messages.
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) for displaying advertisements.

These services have their own privacy policies and security practices.

Advertising

GymX Workout Trainer displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Advertising helps us continue developing, maintaining and improving the application while keeping it free for users. Google may use advertising identifiers and limited device information to provide, measure and improve advertisements in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.
